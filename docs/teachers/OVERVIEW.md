# Teacher Dashboard Overview

The Teacher Dashboard is designed for classroom management and personalized student support.

## 🔑 Key Responsibilities
- **Classroom Oversight**: Monitoring students enrolled in assigned classes.
- **Assignment Cycle**: Creating, distributing, and grading student work.
- **Live Instruction**: Starting and managing Online Jitsi Sessions.
- **Performance Analysis**: Viewing detailed student test results to identify learning gaps.

## 🛠 Feature Breakdown

### 1. Assignment Management
- **Store Assignment**: Teachers can create assignments with titles, descriptions, due dates, and max grades.
- **File Distribution**: Attaching worksheets or reference materials (stored in secure private storage).
- **Submissions View**: A dedicated view per assignment to see who has submitted work, who is late, and who needs grading.

### 2. Grading Workflow
- **Binary Review**: Teachers download student-submitted files via the secure shared download controller.
- **Qualitative Feedback**: Adding comments to submissions.
- **Numeric Assessment**: Entering grades (automatically restricted by the assignment's `max_grade`).

### 3. Online Classroom Coordination
- **Instant Meeting**: Quick-create Jitsi rooms.
- **Session History**: Tracking past meetings and their durations.
- **Status Control**: Closing or deleting sessions once completed.

## 📈 Student Progress Tracking
Teachers can view the **Results Detail** for any student in their class, including AI-generated feedback and the four-skill performance radar.
